FAQ

What is Lightning City?

It's a website (clearly). It serves as a platform for building Lightning Apps. Mainly games (for now at least).

Why does the website only support Bitcoin/Lightning?

I don't really care about anything else.

Whats your plan for Lightning City?

I honestly don't know. At this point I'm experimenting with the 2 things I like the most. Bitcoin and Games.

Why should I register?

The website works perfectly without registering. As soon as you get in an annonnymous account is created for you. However, if you register, you're going to have a username and password to recover your account in case something happens. While not registered you'll only have access to your account within the same machine/browser. The only information needed to register is a username and a password. The password is stored as a hash in the database so there's no way I would be able to know it. The username can be a non identifying name. There's really no privacy loss.

Can I win in the games? What are the odds?

Well, it's a gamble. Some people will win, some will lose. In terms of odds it depends on the game. Generally I point towards 95%. The 5% house cut is what will fund further development.

Should I leave any funds in the website outside of playing sessions?

Not at all. Only keep funds in your wallet while you're playing. Withdraw them right after. I have no intention of stealing funds but you'll have to take my word for it. Also there's a chance the website gets hacked.
